Eight: Ski Free Yeti Chase

On February 23, Crystal Mountain was getting dumped with snow. We were there, ready to shred, but found that most of the mountain was closed for avalanche control. Weak!

Unwilling to give up on a powder day, my brother-in-law Mike and I hiked up a slope to earn a few fresh turns. My wife, Chelsea, videotaped for evidence.

Unfortunately, I ruined the moment with some crappy form.

To make up for this atrocious skiing, I decided to spice up the footage with some classic Ski Free-inspired terror. Having never used them before, I learned a bit of Adobe Premier Pro and Adobe After Effects to give the Abominable Snow Monster life. Much more exciting!

By Alex Marcus
