Tracking a Year of Creativity

On the first day of January in 2020, I resolved to complete one creative endeavor for every week of the year - 52 in total. This site will track my progress, encouraging my new atomic habit.

For the joy of sharing progress and for the accountability of encouraging myself, My Create*52 site will be hosted here, at alexmarc.us/create52.

The code for this site is stored at github.com/alexmarc-us/Create-52. There's more information about this site design in One Please send any requests, tips, for questions to mr@alexmarc.us!
