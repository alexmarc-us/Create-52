Tracking a Year of Creativity

On the first day of January in 2020, I resolved to complete one creative endeavor for every week of the year - 52 in total. To encapsulate that goal, I created the concept of Create*52 - creating one thing for each of the year's 52 weeks.

For the joy of sharing progress and to use publication to encourage myself, my Create*52 progress will be displayed here, at alexmarc.us/create52.

The code for this site is stored at github.com/alexmarc-us/Create-52. Please send any requests, tips, or questions to mr@alexmarc.us!
