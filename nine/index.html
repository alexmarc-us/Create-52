Nine: Eggshells

The Manette Neighborhood Coalition runs an Easter egg hunt every year for the kids in our neighborhood. Since I am the Technology Officer for the MNC, I offered to create a flyer for the event.

Using Adobe Fresco, I had fun while decorating some digital eggshells. Painting real eggshells was a yearly tradition with my mom growing up, so this felt like a modern throw-back.

Each egg was imported into Adobe Photoshop for composition and final placement. I'm excited to see my flyer posted around the neighborhood!

By Alex Marcus
